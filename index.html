Community • Inclusion • Connection

Creating spaces of belonging, care, and human connection.

Rainbow Rose Connect is an NDIS support service focused on social inclusion, capacity building, emotional wellbeing, and community connection. We create personalized, nature-based, and relationship-led experiences that support confidence, independence, creativity, and genuine belonging.

Connect With Us

A softer approach to support.

We believe support should feel human, warm, empowering, and deeply connected. Our approach centers around community, creativity, emotional safety, nature, and meaningful relationships. We create supportive experiences that encourage confidence, self-expression, and joyful participation in everyday life.

What We Offer

Community Connection

Supporting meaningful participation in social and community life through inclusive experiences, outings, and shared connection.

Capacity Building

Encouraging independence, confidence, communication, creativity, and personal growth through relationship-led support.

Nature-Based Wellbeing

Gentle outdoor experiences, grounding activities, creativity, mindfulness, and spaces that support emotional wellbeing.

Our World
