Community • Inclusion • Belonging

We create connection, confidence, and genuine belonging.

Rainbow Rose Connect is an NDIS support service focused on social inclusion, capacity building, wellbeing, and meaningful community connection. We create personalized, nature-based, and relationship-led experiences that support growth, creativity, confidence, and emotional wellbeing.

Get In Touch

A softer approach to support.

We believe support should feel warm, empowering, human, and deeply connected. Our approach centers around nature, creativity, inclusion, emotional safety, and meaningful relationships. We create supportive spaces where individuals feel seen, celebrated, encouraged, and connected to community.

What We Offer

Social Inclusion

Creating opportunities for connection, participation, friendship, and joyful shared experiences.

Capacity Building

Supporting confidence, independence, communication, creativity, and everyday life skills.

Nature Based Wellbeing

Grounding experiences in nature that nurture calm, confidence, creativity, and emotional wellbeing.
