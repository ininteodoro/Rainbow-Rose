Rainbow Rose Connect is an NDIS support service focused on creating meaningful community connection, confidence, creativity, wellbeing, and genuine belonging through relationship-led support.

Connect With Us

A softer way to support.

We believe support should feel warm, personal, creative, and genuinely human. Our approach is centred around kindness, connection, inclusion, and creating spaces where people feel seen, valued, safe, and empowered to grow.

How we support.

Community Connection

Creating opportunities for friendship, participation, social outings, and meaningful shared experiences.

Nature-Based Wellbeing

Encouraging calm, creativity, grounding, and emotional wellbeing through nature and gentle experiences.

Creative Experiences

Supporting self-expression and confidence through art, gardening, music, creativity, and joyful activities.

Daily Living Support

Personalised support with routines, independence, wellbeing, and creating comfort in everyday life.

Capacity Building

Helping build confidence, communication, life skills, independence, and community participation.

Relationship-Led Care

Support built on trust, kindness, consistency, genuine relationships, and emotional safety.
